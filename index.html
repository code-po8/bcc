Welcome to My BBB Projects.

My BBB Projects is a place to devulge on some projects I want to create for the Beaglebone Black. So far I have in mind, a Beer Brew Chamber Controller, that does more than just control temperature.

It will have a database of beer styles and yeasts to chose from that will be used to select the proper temperatures for each stage of the fermentation process. The database will also store a history of brew sessions. Graphs can be made from the data also, Google Charts?

It will also have an alarm system that will send email or text messages when conditions are outside the set parameters.
